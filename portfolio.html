The Challenge

Power Media Solutions needed consistent, professional social media content across multiple client accounts — including a PR firm, a local magazine, and a nonprofit organization. Each client required platform-appropriate content, strategic scheduling, and brand-aligned graphics that would drive engagement and support their marketing goals.

My Approach

I took full ownership of social media management for all three accounts, building a comprehensive content calendar, creating custom graphics and video content, and scheduling everything strategically through Meta Business Suite. I worked independently to make platform decisions, timing calls, and creative choices — ensuring every post aligned with each client's unique voice and objectives.
