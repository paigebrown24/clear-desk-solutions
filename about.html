My Story

Why I started Clear Desk Solutions

Running a business is exhilarating. It's also exhausting.

I watched brilliant, hardworking entrepreneurs drown in the daily details of running their businesses. Emails piling up. Schedules falling apart. Social media going quiet. Books getting messy. Not because they didn't care — but because there simply weren't enough hours in the day.

Over five years ago, I noticed something troubling in the business owner community around me. People who had built incredible things from scratch were spending more time on admin tasks than on the work they actually loved. They needed support — real, reliable, human support.

That's exactly what Clear Desk Solutions was built to provide.

I started this business because I saw a gap. Not just in the market — but in the quality of care being offered to small business owners. Too many services were cold, automated, and impersonal. I wanted to do things differently. I wanted to bring a human touch to every task, every email, every interaction.

Five years later, that's still exactly what I do — every single day.
